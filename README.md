## Requirements
To be eligible for the tournament, you must have always been rated Emerald 1 or below <i>for Season 10</i> on MCSR Ranked. You must also have a Forfeit rate of `15%` or less (rounding down) at the time of registration.

## Tournament Format
The tournament will follow a style similar to the MCSR Ranked Playoffs, but instead starting with a qualifying stage.
### Qualifier Stage
All 32 players compete together in a series of 4 rounds. After 4 rounds, the top 8 players by points advance to bracket.
- Each round, all players are placed on the same seed simultaneously.
- The seed finishes `10 minutes` after the first completion.
- Top 5 earn 6 down to 2 points respectively, and everyone else who finishes gets 1.
- Tiebreakers will be resolved by playing one final 5th game. For example, if there are 2 slots left in Top 8, the first two completions will qualify.
### Bracket
The top 8 players from the qualifier advance to a single-elimination bracket.

**Quarterfinals — First to 1**
- Both players are shown previews of 3 random seeds.
- Player 1 (determined by qualifier points, or MCSR Ranked elo if tied) bans a seed, then Player 2 bans a seed.
- The remaining seed is played.

**Semifinals — Best of 3**
- Both players are shown previews of 5 random seeds.
- Player 1 bans a seed, Player 2 bans a seed, then Player 1 picks the first map to be played.
- After each game, the loser of that game picks the next seed to be played.
- The remaining seed is played as the decider if the series reaches game 3.

**Grand Finals — Best of 5**
- Both players are given 5 random seeds with no preview.
- Seeds are played in order.
